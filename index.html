What is SocialFilm?


SocialFilm is a platform that will create a connection between independent film content creators who want to spread their content in order to satisfy the need of their viewers.





For Content Creators

Content creators will be able to connect with viewers and spread their content. We will achieve this with our content recommendation algorithm through category segmentation.

For Viewers

Viewers will be able to watch indie movies for free, and if they like the content they're watching, they can make a donation and get some exclusive content for that act./p>
